How to Use the Site

1. Create a backlog to add games that you have not completed yet. This will help you track your gaming progress.

2. If you have receipts of your game purchases, log them to get a clear estimate of how much you have really spent.

3. For each game, round up the "HowLongToBeat" time to the nearest hour as a whole number. You can find this information on Google or other sources.

Avoiding the Sunk Cost Fallacy

What is the sunk cost fallacy? It refers to the tendency to continue investing time, effort, or resources into something (in this case, gaming) simply because you have already invested a lot.

Remember, gaming should be enjoyable and for your personal entertainment. Don't feel obligated to continue playing a game just because you've invested a significant amount of time or money into it. If a game is no longer bringing you joy, it's okay to move on and explore new gaming experiences. That's why there is always a DELETE option in my webpage!

Focus on the present and choose games that truly engage and entertain you. Your gaming experience should be driven by enjoyment rather than the fear of wasting previous investments.

With that said, happy gaming and let's get to clearing that backlog!
